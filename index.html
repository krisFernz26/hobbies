What?

This is a website Kyle made for his CC14 (Web Development) course.

The focus for this website is to show the personality of its developer. I, Kyle Fernandez, think that the best way to represent me in a website is something simple. A page that only has 2 colors (green and black) will show that. And having minimal links will provide a cleaner look to it.

The site features a single path flow, having links be continuous downwards, while having a choice to go back to the very top (landing page).

The addition of GIFs in these pages shows that Kyle likes to put a little humor into his sites.

Kyle hopes you enjoy navigating this site as he enjoyed designing it.
